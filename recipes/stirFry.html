Stir Fry

Description

Beef stir fry is a dish made by quickly cooking pieces of beef and vegetables in a wok or large frying pan over high heat. The ingredients are usually sliced or chopped into small, bite-sized pieces, and are cooked with a small amount of oil and a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef, such as sirloin or flank steak
  • A selection of vegetables, such as bell peppers, onions, carrots, and broccoli
  • A wok or large frying pan
  • A small amount of oil, such as vegetable or peanut oil
  • A stir fry sauce, which can be store-bought or homemade

Recipe

  1. To start, heat the oil in the wok or frying pan over high heat. Add the beef and stir fry for a few minutes until it is browned on the outside. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.
  2. Next, add the vegetables to the pan and stir fry them until they are tender-crisp. You can add a little more oil if needed. Return the beef to the pan and add the stir fry sauce. Stir everything together to coat the ingredients in the sauce.
  3. Serve the beef stir fry hot, over a bed of rice or noodles. You can also garnish it with chopped green onions or sesame seeds, if desired.
